Stars of Dust — Project Ezekiel:37

Stars of Dust is a journey of stepping away from worldly illusions to seek God’s truth. Through personal reflections, spiritual insights, and the honest portrayal of human flaws, this project chronicles revival in the valley of dry bones, showing how light rises amid fallen stars.
