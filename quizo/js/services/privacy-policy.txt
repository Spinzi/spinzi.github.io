PRIVACY POLICY FOR QUIZO
Last Updated: July 2026

Quizo ("the platform") is a quiz creation and gameplay application developed by Spinzi. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information based on the platform architecture referenced in bundle.txt.

By using Quizo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect minimal information to provide and maintain the quiz hosting services. The data collected depends on how you interact with Quizo:

A. Quiz Creators (Teachers / Hosts)
To create and manage quizzes, you must log in via Google Authentication. We collect:
* Your unique Firebase Authentication User ID (UID).
* Your display name.
* Your email address.
* Your Google profile photo URL.
* Timestamps of your account creation and most recent login.

B. Quiz Participants (Players)
Players do not create accounts but must provide a nickname to join a lobby. We collect:
* The nickname you choose (which must be at least 4 characters long).
* Your active status within the quiz room.
* Your question responses, points log, and final leaderboard scores.


2. HOW YOUR DATA IS USED

Your data is processed strictly to run the core functionalities of the application:
* To authenticate quiz creators and map saved quizzes back to their specific accounts.
* To generate unique 6-character quiz codes for player lobbies.
* To track real-time question responses and calculate points.
* To render live end-of-game leaderboards for participants.


3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & INFRASTRUCTURE

Quizo utilizes Google Firebase to handle platform infrastructure securely. The specific sub-processors used include:
* Firebase Authentication: To manage secure Google sign-ins for creators.
* Cloud Firestore: To save quiz templates (titles, questions, answers) and player point logs.
* Firebase Analytics: To monitor general performance metrics and anonymous application engagement.


4. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

* Quiz Data: Quiz creators have full control over their content. When a creator deletes a quiz from their dashboard, its questions, answers, and associated player scoring history are permanently erased from the Cloud Firestore database.
* User Accounts: Creator profiles persist until an account deletion request is processed.
